Car appearance customization

Now let's have some fun with customizing the appearance. When we generated our AI agent, it created two rgb files: car1-trb1.rgb and logo.rgb. Those two files are respectively used as skin texture for our car and our pit. You can open both files with an image editing software such as GIMP (recommended), and modify the image while keeping the right proportions.

Thankfully there is another file car1-trb1.xcf which is a GIMP project file that allows us to customize the car skin more effectively.
